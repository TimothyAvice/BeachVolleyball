Thank You

Thank you for signing up and becoming a member of our club. We hold weekly games for each age division at our on location courts, at 123 Pokemund Street, Cairns. At the start of each game you will be required to go to the front desk to aquire an entrance ticket for that game, this is where the game fee will be paid and refunded if neccesary.
